Evaluación

Ejercicios de autoevaluación

Calcule los siguientes límites.

 

1. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{\text{sen}(x)}{9x}}\)  R/ \(\dfrac{1}{9}\)
2. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{\cos(-3x)}{\cos(2x)}}\)   R/no existe
3. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{\text{sen}(x)}{4+\cos(x)}}\)   R/ \(0\)
4. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{\tan(x)}{5x}}\) R/ \(\dfrac{1}{5}\)
5. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{1+\text{sen}(x)}{1+\cos(x)}}\) R/\(\dfrac{1}{2}\)
6. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{1}{x\sec(x)\csc(x)}}\)   R/\(1\)
7. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 1}\dfrac{\text{sen}(x-1)}{3x-3}}\)     R/\(\dfrac{1}{3}\)
8. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{\text{sen}^2(5x)}{x^2}}\)    R/\(25\)
9. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}6x\cot(3x)}\)     R/\(2\)
10. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 2}\dfrac{x-2}{\text{sen}(x)}}\)   R/\(0\)
11. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\dfrac{\cos(x)-1}{\cos^2(x)-1}}\)         
 R/\(\dfrac{1}{2}\)
12. \(\displaystyle{\lim_{x\rightarrow \frac{\pi}{4}}\dfrac{\cos(2x)}{\cos(x)-\text{sen}(x)}}\)    
 R/\(\sqrt{2}\)
